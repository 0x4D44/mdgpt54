## Known Risks / Future Work

- Status pill text may be overwritten by MapLibre's `idle` event between toggle click and assertion. The `retries: 1` config and Playwright's auto-retry mitigate this.
- `page.routeWebSocket` is Chromium-only. Suite locked to Chromium via config.
- DEM protocol tiles can't be intercepted by `page.route()` — terrain elevation always returns null in tests. This is documented and handled.
- Console noise from MapLibre on empty tile/font responses is expected and ignorable.
